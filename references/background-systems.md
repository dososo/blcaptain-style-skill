# Background and Texture Systems

v1.1 formalizes texture as a design token layer.

Textures are not decorative backgrounds. They support system identity, image integration, and readability.

## Still Paper texture tokens

### paper-warm-grain

Use with:

- SP-02 Warm Study
- creator diary
- reading note
- desk / coffee / home

Effect:

- warm paper grain
- soft print feeling
- low contrast

Avoid:

- screenshot-heavy cards
- dark dashboards

---

### paper-cool-mist

Use with:

- SP-03 Coastal Quiet
- travel / sea / lake / misty landscape

Effect:

- cool soft paper
- light haze
- airy editorial feeling

Avoid:

- high-contrast data card
- food / warm table

---

### paper-fiber-soft

Use with:

- SP-01 Mist Field
- outdoor / field notes / non-fiction

Effect:

- visible fiber
- tactile documentary feel
- natural surface

Avoid:

- product dashboard
- data-heavy dense layout

---

### night-grain

Use with:

- SP-04 Night Grain
- film / game / night city

Effect:

- low-light paper grain
- cinematic black
- subtle gold or amber accent

Avoid:

- small gray text
- dense body copy

---

### table-linen

Use with:

- SP-05 Hearth & Table
- food / coffee / home / object study

Effect:

- soft fabric / table surface
- warm still-life feeling

Avoid:

- technical screenshots
- high-density comparison table

---

---

### screen-matte

Use with:

- screenshot-heavy layouts
- product UI walkthroughs

Effect:

- matte surface
- subdued dashboard contrast
- screenshot reads as evidence

Avoid:

---

### data-grid-soft

Use with:

- SL-03 Safety Coral
- KPI / data recap

Effect:

- subtle data grid
- structured without coldness

Avoid:

- overly busy chart backgrounds

---

### noir-carbon

Use with:

- SL-05 Signal Noir candidate studies only
- dark data / game / premium technical topics after board approval

Effect:

- carbon-like dark texture
- low-glow chart and UI elements

Avoid:

- low-contrast amber body text
- casual lifestyle photos
- production use before the Signal Noir visual direction board is approved

## Full-bleed image overlay tokens

### image-soft-mask

General soft readability layer.

### image-bottom-fade

Use when title or footer sits near image bottom.

### image-side-paper-card

Use when text sits beside subject and needs paper-like readable surface.

### image-subject-safe-zone

Reserve area around product / food / landscape subject.

### image-face-avoid-zone

Do not place title over face, eyes, hands, product logo, dense UI, or food focal area.

## Rules

1. Every image-led card declares one texture token.
2. Every full-bleed image declares one overlay token.
3. Every screenshot card declares one screenshot stage token.
4. Do not use more than two texture layers in one card.
5. Texture must never reduce text readability.
6. Texture does not replace image quality.
